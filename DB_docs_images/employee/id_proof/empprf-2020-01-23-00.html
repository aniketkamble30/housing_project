Quick guide to Bh.Papers

  • Bh.Papers are not just ordinary past year university question papers, We are providing you with 4 elements of each paper
  • 1st portion displaying the past year question paper (M.U) "Question Paper May 2017" is the tag name followed by next paper.
  • 2nd section reveals the exact marks distribution as per Module in terms of percentage. "10% Module 6 means 10% of total marks of paper was appeared from module 6" and so on for all the modules. 
  • 3rd portion gives you total number of questions from each module. "35% Module 1 means 35% of total number of questions (7 questions) was from module 1" and similar calculation for all the modules is followed.â â
  • 4th is Color concept gives you clear idea about the repetition of questions:
  • âBlue - Question repeated 1 time, Green - 2 times, Orange - 3 times, Pink - 4 times and Red - Now Not in syllabus.
